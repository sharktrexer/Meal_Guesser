Welcome to Guess That Meal!
In this game you will have to guess the name of 10 meals just by their image. You have 3 chances to check how many points your guess earns before submitting a final answer. Every correct word in your guess that corresponds with a word in the meal name results in 1 point. This DOES include conjunctions like "with" or "and" so guess away! At the end of the game you will get your final score and how many points you could have obtained. Press the button below to start. Have fun!
